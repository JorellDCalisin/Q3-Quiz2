Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande, born June 26, 1993, in Boca Raton, Florida, U.S., is a versatile American artist celebrated for her remarkable talent as a singer, songwriter, and actress. She rose to prominence through her role as Cat Valentine in the Nickelodeon television series Victorious (2010–2013) and later in the spin-off Sam & Cat (2013–2014).

Grande embarked on her musical journey with the release of the soundtrack Music from Victorious (2011), marking the inception of her illustrious career. Her debut album, Yours Truly, released in 2013, soared to the top of the US Billboard 200, catapulting Grande into stardom.

Throughout her illustrious career, Grande has garnered widespread acclaim, earning numerous prestigious awards, including a Grammy Award, a Brit Award, three American Music Awards, three MTV Europe Music Awards, and two MTV Video Music Awards.
